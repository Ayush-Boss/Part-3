DO I NEED TO WORK OUT EVERY DAY?

No, you do not need to work out every day. In fact, in most cases, I would recommend at least 1-2 days of total rest a week. However, just because you have a rest or recovery day scheduled into your calendar, does necessarily mean you aren active at all on these days.

HOW LONG SHOULD I WORK OUT FOR?

In general, your workouts do not need to be long to be effective. Many people who are new to fitness imagine slogging around for hours on the treadmill. This doesnt need to be the case. The length of your workout will largely depend on your fitness level, as well as the type.

HOW OFTEN SHOULD I REST?

Your rest will depend on your workout schedule. Like I said previously, I recommend 1 or 2 full days of rest in your schedule. This isnt only great for giving your body time to recover for your workouts, it also can be a great time for self-care to promote mental health.

HOW LONG I TAKE TO SEE RESULTS?

It can take anywhere from 6-8 weeks or more to see results from your program. That can be discouraging as so many of us are living in an instant world - we expect results and changes instantaneously. Dont get discouraged. Know that your hard work is paying off.
